EXAMINATION REGULATIONS 2024

Eligibility for Examinations:
Students must meet attendance requirements and have no outstanding fees.

Re-examination Policy:
Students who fail a course may apply for re-examination in the next semester.

Use of Unfair Means:
Any student caught cheating will receive an F grade and may face disciplinary action.

Exam Schedule:
Final exam schedules will be published at least four weeks before the examination period.
