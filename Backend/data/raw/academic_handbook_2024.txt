UNIVERSITY ACADEMIC HANDBOOK 2024

Attendance Requirements:
Students must maintain a minimum of 75% attendance in each course to be eligible to appear for examinations.

Attendance Relaxation:
Attendance may be relaxed up to 65% for students with valid medical reasons supported by official documentation.

Course Registration:
Students must register for courses within the first two weeks of the semester.

Credit Requirements:
Undergraduate students must complete a minimum of 120 credits to graduate.
Postgraduate students must complete 60 credits.

Grading System:
Grades range from A+ to F. A grade below C is considered a fail for core courses.
