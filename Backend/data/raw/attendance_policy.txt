ATTENDANCE POLICY

Minimum Attendance:
Students are required to maintain at least 75% attendance in all courses.

Shortage of Attendance:
Students with attendance between 65% and 75% may be allowed to appear for exams with departmental approval.

Below 65% Attendance:
Students with attendance below 65% will not be permitted to sit for examinations.

Medical Leave:
Certified medical leave may be considered when calculating attendance.
